Once you log in to your FedaPay dashboard, the **Dashboard** page provides an instant overview of your financial activity.

This interface is designed to help you monitor your performance at a glance using clear and dynamic indicators.

### Key Statistics

At the top of the page, youâ€™ll find three main indicators that summarize your recent activity:

- **Gross Collection Volume**
 Displays the total amount collected over the selected period.

- **Number of Refunds**
 Indicates the total number of refunds processed during the period.

- **Successful Collections**
 Shows the volume of successfully completed transactions.

### Payment Information

Further down, the interface provides a detailed analysis of your payments, broken down by payment method (bank card, mobile money, etc.).
You have access to:

- **Volume by Payment Method**
 Compare the amounts collected through each method. Ideal for identifying top-performing channels.

- **Number of Payments by Method**
 View how often each payment method is used.

Data can be filtered over different periods:
 **Yesterday, last 7 days, last month, or current year.**
